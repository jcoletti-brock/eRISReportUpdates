# Business Analyst Workflow

## Communication and Research

### Research Guidelines

- **Technology Validation**: Research proposed technologies for viability, maturity, and community support
- **Industry Standards**: Verify compliance with relevant industry standards and best practices
- **Competitive Analysis**: Research similar solutions to validate approach and timeline
- **Cost Validation**: Verify pricing for licenses, services, and third-party components
- **Timeline Realism**: Compare proposed timelines against industry benchmarks
- **Reference Projects**: Find similar project examples to inform estimates

### Stakeholder Communication

- **Ask Clarifying Questions**: When requirements are unclear, list specific questions
- **Present Options**: Offer alternatives with pros/cons when multiple approaches exist
- **Flag Concerns Early**: Identify potential issues proactively
- **Confirm Understanding**: Summarize key points to ensure alignment
- **Document Decisions**: Record all decisions and their rationale

### Iterative Refinement

- Work on one section at a time to maintain focus
- Request feedback on completed sections before moving forward
- Refine based on stakeholder input
- Update cross-references when changes are made
- Maintain a change log for significant revisions

## Tools and Resources

- **Research Tools**: Web searches for technology validation, industry research, and best practices
- **Visualization**: Mermaid diagrams for architecture, timelines, and workflows
- **Validation**: Cross-reference with technical documentation and standards
- **Collaboration**: Engage with technical experts to validate feasibility
